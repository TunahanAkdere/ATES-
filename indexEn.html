    ATEŞ İNŞAAT

    Building the Future with Trust and Quality

    Ateş İnşaat was established as a sole proprietorship by Abdullah Ateş on February 20, 1973. Since its inception, it has undertaken various infrastructure projects, particularly in construction and sewage works. It transformed into a Limited Company on March 27, 1987, and became a Joint Stock Company on May 23, 2016.
