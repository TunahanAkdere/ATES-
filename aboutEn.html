    Building the Future with Trust and Quality

    1973 - Establishment

    Ateş İnşaat was established as a sole proprietorship by Abdullah Ateş on February 20, 1973. Since its inception, it has undertaken various infrastructure projects, particularly in drinking water and sewage works.

    February 20, 1973

    1987 - Transition to Limited Company

    It transitioned to a Limited Company on March 27, 1987.

    March 27, 1987

    2016 - Becoming a Joint Stock Company

    It became a Joint Stock Company on May 23, 2016.

    May 23, 2016

    Management Changes

    Since 2016, Cevdet Ateş has been appointed as the Chairman of the Board of Directors, and Enes Ateş has been appointed as a Board Member.

    2016 - 2024
